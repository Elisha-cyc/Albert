Course Description

This comprehensive course provides the foundational knowledge and hands-on skills needed for residential electrical wiring. Designed for beginners, the program covers all aspects of basic electrical systems, safety protocols, and code requirements.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand electrical theory and basic circuitry
  • Identify and properly use electrical tools and materials
  • Install and repair common residential wiring systems
  • Apply National Electrical Code (NEC) requirements
  • Practice safe working habits with electricity

Who Should Attend

  • Aspiring electricians seeking career entry
  • Homeowners wanting to understand their electrical systems
  • Handymen looking to expand their service offerings

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Electrical Fundamentals (8 hours)

  • Introduction to electricity
  • Basic electrical theory (voltage, current, resistance)
  • Types of circuits (series, parallel, combination)
