Product Description

The Professional Voltage Tester is designed for electricians, technicians, and DIY enthusiasts who need reliable voltage detection. Its rugged construction and advanced features make it suitable for both residential and commercial applications.

Features include non-contact voltage detection for added safety, dual-range detection (12V-1000V), bright LED indicators for clear visibility in all lighting conditions, and an audible alarm for noisy environments. The IP65 rating ensures protection against dust and water jets, making it suitable for use in various conditions.
