About Us

Your trusted partner for electrical solutions and training

Our Story

Founded in 2005, ElectroMaster began as a small electrical supply store with a vision to provide quality products and exceptional service to both professionals and DIY enthusiasts. Over the years, we've grown into a comprehensive electrical solutions provider, adding professional training programs to our offerings in 2012.

What started as a family-owned business has now become a regional leader in electrical supplies and education, serving thousands of customers and students each year. Our commitment to quality, safety, and education remains at the core of everything we do.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality electrical products and professional training that empower individuals and businesses to work safely and efficiently with electrical systems.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of electrical solutions and education, recognized for our expertise, quality products, and commitment to safety.

Our Values

  • Quality in products and services
  • Commitment to safety
  • Continuous learning and improvement
  • Customer satisfaction
  • Community engagement
